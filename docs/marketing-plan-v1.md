# Marketing Plan — V1

> Last updated: 2026-04-01
> Target: V-Health Rehab Clinic (Richmond/Vancouver, BC, Canada)

---

## Strategy: Clinic-First, Patient-Follows

We don't market to patients directly in V1. We partner with V-Health, and they introduce the tool to their patients. Our "marketing" is making V-Health successful, then using that as a case study to land the next clinic.

---

## Phase 1: V-Health Pilot (Month 1-2)

### Goal
Enroll 20-30 V-Health patients into the AI recovery coach.

### Tactics

**For the clinic owner:**
- Position as a competitive advantage: "You're the first clinic in the area to offer AI recovery coaching"
- Zero cost to V-Health during pilot (we absorb SMS costs)
- We build it white-labeled under vhealth.ai — it's THEIR tool, not ours
- Provide marketing copy they can use: website banner, in-clinic signage, social media posts

**For practitioners (Kyle, Alice, etc.):**
- 15-minute demo showing the dashboard and pre-appointment summaries
- Frame it as: "your patients will come in with data instead of 'I feel about the same'"
- No extra work required — patients self-log; AI generates summaries
- Set up their alert preferences (inactive patients, anomaly thresholds)

**For patients (at the clinic):**
- Front desk introduces it during checkout: "Would you like to try our new recovery coach? It helps track your progress between visits."
- Simple signup: name, phone number, condition — that's it for day 1
- First SMS sent immediately: "Hi [name], I'm your V-Health recovery coach. How are you feeling after today's session?"
- Profile built over first week of conversation (AI asks gradually, not a long form)

### Materials Needed
- Printed card / QR code for front desk (links to vhealth.ai signup)
- 1-page explainer for practitioners
- Social media post template for V-Health accounts
- In-clinic poster or table tent

---

## Phase 2: Validate & Optimize (Month 2-3)

### Goal
Prove that the tool drives measurable patient engagement and practitioner satisfaction.

### Key Questions to Answer
1. What % of enrolled patients are still logging after 4 weeks?
2. Do practitioners actually look at the dashboard before appointments?
3. What's the rebooking rate difference between coached and non-coached patients?
4. What do patients say they like/dislike?
5. What do practitioners want that's missing?

### Tactics
- Weekly check-in with V-Health owner (15 min)
- Short survey to patients at 2-week and 4-week marks
- Track all metrics defined in product-scope.md
- Iterate on AI prompts based on real conversation logs

---

## Phase 3: Case Study & Outreach (Month 3-4)

### Goal
Use V-Health results to approach 3-5 additional clinics.

### Tactics

**Build the case study:**
- "V-Health increased patient rebooking by X% with AI recovery coaching"
- Include real metrics (anonymized): engagement rates, practitioner feedback, patient testimonials
- Before/after comparison: how practitioners used to work vs. now

**Outreach to local clinics:**
- Target: RMT clinics, acupuncture practices, physiotherapy clinics in Greater Vancouver
- Channel: Cold email/visit with case study
- Offer: Same free pilot model — 30 days free, then monthly subscription
- Leverage V-Health as reference ("call Kyle, ask him how it works")

**Pricing for post-pilot clinics:**
- Monthly SaaS fee per clinic (not per patient)
- Suggested range: $99-199/month for small clinic (<5 practitioners)
- Includes white-label branding, SMS costs (within limits), dashboard access
- Volume pricing TBD based on demand

---

## What We Don't Do in V1
- No paid advertising
- No social media marketing under our own brand
- No consumer-facing marketing
- No PR / press outreach
- No app store listing

**V1 is 100% partner-driven distribution.** The clinic is our marketing channel.

---

## Key Messages

**For clinic owners:**
> "Keep your patients engaged between appointments. They log their recovery via text, you see their progress on a dashboard. Your clinic, your brand."

**For practitioners:**
> "Know how your patient is really doing before they sit down. Real data, not guesswork."

**For patients (said by the clinic):**
> "We now offer a recovery coach that checks in on you between visits. Just text us how you're feeling."
